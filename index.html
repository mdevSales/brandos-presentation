How This Shows Up at Work

Internal Visibility

  • High-impact projects: Leadership sees your work when looking for people to tap
  • Cross-team collaboration: People look you up before reaching out to work together
  • Peer credibility: Colleagues see your expertise and what you've shipped
  • Company-wide recognition: Your wins become visible beyond your immediate team

External Opportunities

  • Inbound recruiting: Recruiters find you before you're even looking
  • Speaking invitations: Conference organizers discover your expertise
  • Network expansion: Industry peers connect based on your content
  • Side opportunities: Consulting, advisory roles, partnerships surface naturally

Bottom line: A strong LinkedIn presence compounds over time. Every post, every profile view, every connection builds momentum that creates opportunities you didn't even know existed.

The Challenge

Most people struggle with:

  • Not knowing what makes them different
  • Generic profiles that sound like everyone else
  • Not knowing what to post about
  • Feeling overwhelmed by LinkedIn
  • AI-generated content that sounds fake
  • No clear plan to build momentum
