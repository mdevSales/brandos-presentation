External Opportunities

  • Speaking invitations: Conference organizers discover your expertise
  • Network expansion: Industry peers connect based on your content
  • Side opportunities: Consulting, advisory roles, partnerships surface naturally

The real challenge

Most people struggle with three core problems:

The Identity Problem

I don't know what makes me different. My profile sounds like everyone else's.

The Voice Problem

I don't want to sound fake, corporate, or AI-generated. I want to sound like myself.

The Consistency Problem

I don't know what to post after day one. I run out of ideas immediately.

Talk track: "If you've felt stuck on LinkedIn, it's probably for one of these three reasons. You don't know what makes you different. You're afraid of sounding fake. And you have no idea what to post consistently. Sound familiar? Good. That's exactly what we're fixing today."
